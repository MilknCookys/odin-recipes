How to make Iced Tea

Description:

Iced Tea is a variation on the classic drink of tea, and is typically made without milk. People commonly flavour it with fruit, however this recipe is just for the base drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 tea bag or equivalent
  • 200ml water, boiled
  • 1Tbsp caster sugar
  • 1tsp lemon juice
  • 150ml ice

Steps

  1. Steep the tea bag in the water for 6 minutes
  2. Take the tea bag out, and mix in the sugar
  3. Add lemon juice
  4. Add ice until the total volume of the drink reaches 350ml
  5. Stir for about 30 seconds
  6. Enjoy!
